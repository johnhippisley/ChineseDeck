# ChineseDeck

A media-rich Anki deck for learning Chinese vocabulary.

---

## Features

- Pinyin
- Audio
- Example sentences
- Images
- Stroke order animations
- 5,716 of the most common words and patterns

## Tags

Cards are organized into various vocabulary lists, including chapters from Chinese Link Part 1 & 2 and All Things Considered. They are also organized into HSK 1–6 and several other categories.

I recommend starting with all cards suspended, then gradually unsuspending cards as you go.
